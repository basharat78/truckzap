About Our Agency

Truck Zap Dispatching LLC:
Your Reliable Partner

Truck Zap Dispatching LLC is an independent truck dispatch company specializing in helping small trucking companies with 1 to 5 units. Our team of professionals is dedicated to providing high-quality dispatch services across the USA, ensuring that owner-operators and small fleets can focus on driving while we handle the logistics.
